## Ingredients

* 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
* 3 garlic cloves, minced
* ⅓ cup ketchup
* ⅓ cup rice syrup
* ¼ cup gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
* 2 teaspoons white vinegar
* 2 teaspoons vegetable oil

Mix together on heat until it comes to boil.
